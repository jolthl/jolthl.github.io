Hello! I am Jolin Teo, a 21 year old Singaporean, studying for a Computer Science degree at Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). I am passionate about design and how it interacts with humans in our daily lives and I am constantly learning new skills to help improve what I want to achieve. I am interested in UI/UX and would be open to talking about it more.

I am open to internships in any roles related to user interface and experience design to hone my skills further! My current skills are design in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Canva and a beginner level of Figma. I also enjoy editing music and videos on Premiere Pro. While coding may not be my strongest feat, I am well versed in Python, Java and HTML. I am passionate and am always ready to learn new things. Feel free to contact me either through email, LinkedIn or Telegram!

Other than designing and work, I am also passionate about dance. I had started dancing in my current dance club, the SUTD Hip Hop club, Dance DerivativeZ. I find the time spent in my Fifth Row very fruitful and a way for me to relax and have a break from school. Whenever I have more time and lesser dance events in school, I participate in external dance events and classes as well, such as L4D and external recitals by dance companies.
