Dock & Block Video

Dock & Block is an SUTD Term 2, 3.007 Design Thinking and Innovation module. This project was created to maximise the seats in a hawker centre (mainly focusing on Amoy Street as our target audience), especially during the COVID-19 period. There was a lack of seats due to the social distancing measures required.

This video was in the animal crossing x Jojo's Bizarre Adventures themed, to make the subject a bit more lighthearted, especially needed during tough times in COVID-19.
